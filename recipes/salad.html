Simple Salad

This simple salad is a delicious and refreshing combination of crisp cucumbers, creamy avocado, juicy tomatoes, and a few other basic ingredients. Here's how to make it:

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the sliced cucumber, diced avocado, diced tomato, thinly sliced red onion, and chopped fresh parsley.
  2. In a small mixing bowl, whisk together the olive oil and red wine vinegar. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!

This salad is not only delicious, but it's also full of healthy fats and vitamins. The avocado adds creaminess and healthy fats, while the cucumber and tomato provide a refreshing crunch. The red onion adds a bit of zing, while the parsley adds freshness and a pop of green color. Overall, this salad is a perfect side dish for any meal or can be enjoyed on its own for a light lunch or snack.
