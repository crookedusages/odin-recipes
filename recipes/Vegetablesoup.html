Vegetable Soup

Not only is this soup delicious but and it’s so easy to make. It just takes some chopping and a bit of simmering until you’ve got a soup everyone will love! This is a recipe you’ll definitely want to add to your dinner or lunch rotation. And it yields a pot full and makes great leftovers!

Ingredients

  • medium onion
  • celery
  • olive oil
  • carrots
  • medium potatoes
  • tomato paste
  • vegetable stock or chicken broth
  • canned diced tomatoes
  • frozen corn
  • frozen green beans
  • frozen peas
  • Salt and pepper

Directions

  1. In a soup pot, heat oil and add onion, celery and carrots. Saute till veggies start to become tender and onions are translucent.
  2. Add potatoes and tomato paste and stir till coated well. Add stock or broth (depending on what you use) followed by tomatoes, corn, beans, and peas.
  3. Allow soup to come to a low simmer and cook for about 15-20 minutes or until all veggies are tender. Season with salt and pepper. Ladle into bowls and serve with crusty bread.
