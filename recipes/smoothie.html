Super simple smoothie

Smoothies are a delicious and simple way to get your day started with a bunch of nutritious foods in your belly. Here's one of my go to smoothie recipes:

Ingredients

  • ripe banana
  • frozen/fresh blueberries
  • spinach
  • half a ripe avocado
  • water/milk/milk alternative

Directions

  1. place all the ingredients in the cup
  2. blend it
  3. add more liquid and blend it again if the mixture is too thick
